## 2. Information We Collect

Our App is designed to provide real-time gold price information while maintaining your privacy. We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or financial details.

### 2.1 Device Information

We automatically collect non-personal device information, including:
- Device type and model

- Device language and locale settings
- Mobile network information

### 2.2 Usage Analytics

We collect information about how you interact with our App, including:
- Features you access and their frequency
- Time spent in the App and specific sections
- Gold price data you view and search queries
- User interface interactions and navigation patterns
- Crash logs and performance metrics

This information is collected to help us improve functionality, optimize user experience, and identify technical issues.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **App Improvement**: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance features and functionality
- **Performance Monitoring**: Tracking app stability, identifying bugs, and measuring performance
- **User Experience Optimization**: Understanding how users interact with the App to streamline navigation and interface design
- **Analytics and Reporting**: Generating aggregated, anonymized reports on app usage trends

All data is processed in aggregated, anonymized form that does not identify you personally.
